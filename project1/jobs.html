G01 - Software Developer

The position demands a highly experienced programmer to join our team in order to develop software based on the company's plan. The programmer must be able to produce software in accordance with each timetable and maintain constant control over the work.

Salary Range: 3000-5000 AUD/ per month

Key Responsibilities:

  • Complete task according to schedule.
  • Communicate effectively with team members and clients.
  • Maintain a positive attitude and spirit when collaborating or working in a team.
  • Always strive for software optimization and perfection.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Skills and Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Software Engineering.
  2. More than 3 years of relevant work experience.
  3. Strong programming skills in at least 3 common programming languages.
    • Python
    • Java
    • C++
  4. Standard knowledge of data structures and algorithms, OOP and Databases.
  5. Familiar in utilizing AI to increase work productivity.

  5. Familiar in utilizing AI to increase work productivity.
